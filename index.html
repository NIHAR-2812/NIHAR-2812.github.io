About Me

My Journey

I'm Nihar Padave, a Second-Year Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) student in Electronics & Computer Science at Shah & Anchor Kutchhi Engineering College, Mumbai (Expected Graduation: 2027).

My academic journey began with a strong foundation in school — scoring 90% in Class 10 (SSC) and 67% in Class 12 (HSC) — followed by securing a 85.03 percentile in MHT-CET, which opened the door to pursue my passion for technology at Shah & Anchor. Currently, I have maintained a GPA of 8.24 in my First Year, reflecting my consistent dedication to both theoretical and practical learning.

My fascination with technology started when I built my very first HTML webpage, sparking a deep curiosity about how systems work behind the scenes. Since then, I’ve developed a keen interest in both hardware and software integration, especially in areas like IoT, Machine Learning, and Digital System Design. Through coursework in Data Structures, Object-Oriented Programming, and Computer Architecture, I’ve strengthened my understanding of how logic and code interact to create real-world impact.

I enjoy building projects that combine innovation with purpose — transforming ideas into efficient, real-world solutions. I’m motivated by challenges that push me to think critically, learn continuously, and contribute meaningfully to the world of technology.
